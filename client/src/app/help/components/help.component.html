Profile

Update Your Profile

  • Click on the ellipsis icon on your profile page.
  • Click on "Edit Profile".
  • Select your current country from the dropdown.
  • Write or update your bio in the text box provided.
  • Upload a new profile picture by selecting an image from your computer.
  • Click "Save" to apply your updates.

View Your Activity

Your recent blog posts, comments, and forum threads will appear in your activity feed.

Delete Your Profile

  • Click on the ellipsis icon on your profile page.
  • Click on "Delete Profile".
  • Wait for the confirmation timer to count down
  • Click "Confirm"

This deletes your profile as well as any blogs, posts, and comments that you have made. This cannot be undone!

Blog

Create a New Blog Post

  • On the Blog page click the "Create a Post" button
  • Add a catchy title and write your content in the editor.
  • Click the "Upload‚Äù button to add images from your device
  • Select categories to classify your post.
  • Click "Create Blog Post" to share your post .

Comment on a Blog Post

  • Open any blog post and scroll to the bottom to find the comments section
  • Type your comment in the text input
  • Click the "Post Comment" button

Search and Filter Blogs

  • On the Blog page use the search bar to find Blogs by keywords or categories.
  • Click the category dropdown and select categories from the menu.
  • Enter keywords into the search box.
  • Click the "Search" button and the results will be displayed below.
  • Clicking the "Reset" button will clear the search and any selected categories.

Edit a Blog Post

  • On a Blog post page you created click on the ellipsis icon
  • Click "Edit" from the menu.
  • Make your changes to the Title, Content, Images, and Categories.
  • Click the "Save" button to confirm the changes

Delete a Blog Post

  • On a Blog post page you created click on the ellipsis icon
  • Click "Delete" from the menu.
  • Wait for the confirmation timer to count down
  • Click "Confirm"

This deletes your blog post and cannot be undone!
